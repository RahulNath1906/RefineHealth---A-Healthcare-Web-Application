All about Corona Virus

be aware, stay safe

how to prevent virus

Sanitize your place

Sanitize where you place where you stay or where you sit in a crowded place.

maintain distance

Maintain atleast 2 ft distance with people and avoid physical contact.

don't touch face

Viruses and other bacterias are contagious and can easily ent our body. So, avoid touching your face as much as you can.

wash your hand

Wash your hands frequently for atleast 20 seconds.

use napkin

Use napkins or tissue to cover your mouth before sneezing and coughing.

wear a mask

Always wear a mask whenever you move out of your home, also change/wash your masks after 2-3 days.

take precautions against covid-19

things you should DO

wash your hand

Wash your hands with atleast for 20 seconds, whenever you are going to eat something or you enter your home.

always wear a mask

Always wear a mask to prevent corona virus from spreading and getting affected.

stay home during fever

Stay at home take rest, don't step out of your home unecessarily. Take your medicines on time.

things you should NOT DO

avoid close contact with animals

Animals can become a means of spreading viruses. So, it's better to avoid close contact with them.

avoid close contact with peoples

As the virus is contagious avoid close contact with people. Maintain a 2 ft distance from them and always wear a mask.

avoid crowded area

Avoid gathering on crowded areas, if you are going to such place, sanitize yourself frequently and don't put off your mask.
